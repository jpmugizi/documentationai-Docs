---
title: Dashboard Not Loading
description: Steps for blank or stuck dashboard screen.
---

## Symptoms
- Dashboard stays blank
- Loading spinner never finishes

## Causes
- Browser cache issue
- Network interruption

## Quick Fix
<Steps>
  <Step title="Refresh page">
    Press Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac) to hard refresh.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Go to browser settings > Privacy > Clear cache.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check internet connection">
    Ensure you are online. Restart router if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try different browser">
    Some plugins may block scripts; try Chrome or Firefox.
  </Step>
</Steps>
