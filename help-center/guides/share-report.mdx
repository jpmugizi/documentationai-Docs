## Share via Link

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your report">
    Navigate to the Reports section and select your saved analysis.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Share">
    In the top right, click the **Share** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy link or enter emails">
    Choose **Copy Link** to share, or enter teammates' emails for invitation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set permissions">
    Decide if viewers can edit or only view. Confirm your choice.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
You can revoke access anytime from the report's Share menu.
</Callout>
