## Overview

Connect your first data source to import and analyze data in Baza Analytics.

## Steps

1. Open the Baza Analytics dashboard.
2. Click **Data Sources** in the sidebar.
3. Select your provider (e.g., Google Sheets, MySQL, CSV).
4. Enter required connection details and credentials.
5. Click **Connect**.
6. Your data will appear and be ready for queries.
